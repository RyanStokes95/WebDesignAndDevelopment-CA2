If you come by car, we have parking

From The Spire, turn left onto Roselawn Street. Continue straight until the roundabout and take the second exit onto Gardenia Road. After about 2 kilometers, you'll see Mick's Garden on your right.

Well serviced by bus options

To reach Mick's Garden store via Dublin Bus, take bus number 123 towards The Spire. Get off at the Gardenia Road stop and walk straight ahead for about 200 meters. Mick's Garden will be seen on your right.

Train option for long distance travellers

If you're traveling to Mick's Garden store by train, take the Dublin-Cork line and get off at the Connolly station. From there, head northwest onto Gardenia Road for approximately 500 meters until you reach Clover Lane. After a short walk, you'll find Mick's Garden on your right.
